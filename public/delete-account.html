Delete your Meridian account data

Meridian lets you play as a guest or with a registered account. Guest progress is temporary and cannot be recovered. For a registered account, you can request deletion of the account and the game data connected to it.

How to make a request

  1. Use the email button below.
  2. Include the exact Meridian username for the account you want deleted.
  3. State that you want your Meridian account and associated game data deleted.
Request account deletion

What will be deleted

After account ownership is confirmed, Meridian will delete the account record and associated stored game profile data, including the username, guild, balances, progression, cosmetic settings, and linked sign-in identifiers. Some aggregated, non-identifying game statistics may remain because they cannot be used to identify you.

Confirmation and timing

For account protection, the developer may ask for reasonable confirmation that you control the account before deleting it. Requests are normally handled within 30 days. If you created an account through Google or Apple, you may be asked to use the email address associated with that sign-in when available.

Questions

Contact support@lietastudios.com. See the Meridian Privacy Policy for more information.
