How to play Meridian

1

Click any unclaimed cell to claim it with Lumen. Click a cell you already own to fortify it -- more defense makes it harder to steal.

2

Follow the dawn: gold rush zones triple your income and halve attack costs. The band sweeps the globe with the real sun.

3

Join a guild to chat, share protection from your own members, and declare wars against rivals for bragging rights and Lumen rewards.

4

Spend Lumen on Shield, Overcharge, and Siege abilities for tactical plays beyond just clicking cells.
