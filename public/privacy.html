Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 14, 2026 · Developer: Hyacinthe Loua (Lieta Studios) · Contact: support@lietastudios.com

1. Overview

Meridian: Race the Dawn ("Meridian") is a free, real-time multiplayer browser game. This policy explains what information Meridian collects, why, and how it's handled. Meridian is built and operated by a single independent developer, not a company with a dedicated data team -- this policy describes the setup as it actually works.

2. Account Information

You can play as a Guest with no account at all, or create a free account with a username and password. Creating an account does not require an email address or any other personal information. Passwords are stored as salted cryptographic hashes -- Meridian never stores or can look up your actual password.

Account data stored includes: your username, password hash and salt, chosen guild (if any), Lumen balance, season and career Lumen totals, chosen cosmetic color, and Founder/Supporter status. This data is used entirely to run the game and is not sold or shared with advertisers.

3. Guild Chat and In-Game Content

Guild chat messages are visible to members of your guild only, and are not persisted beyond a short in-memory history -- they are not permanently archived. Chat is not actively moderated by a human in real time; please don't share personal information in chat.

4. IP Addresses

Meridian temporarily uses your IP address for abuse prevention only -- limiting concurrent connections and rate-limiting signups, logins, and other actions from the same network. This is handled in server memory and is not written to the persistent database or linked to your account identity beyond that immediate throttling purpose.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

Meridian doesn't use advertising or tracking cookies. It stores a few small values in your browser's local storage to make the game work smoothly: a session token (so you don't have to log in every visit), whether you've seen the tutorial, your sound and colorblind-mode preferences, and a randomly generated anonymous ID used only for the analytics described below. None of these identify you personally and none are shared with advertisers.

6. Analytics

Meridian uses TelemetryDeck, a privacy-focused, cookieless analytics service, to understand feature usage in aggregate (for example, how many players use a given ability, or complete the tutorial). Signals sent are tied to a random identifier generated in your browser, not to your username, email, or IP address. Meridian also maintains its own self-hosted, aggregate-only usage statistics (peak concurrent players, claims per day, new accounts per day) that contain no player-identifying information at all.

7. Advertising

Meridian does not currently display any ads. A placeholder for a future ad slot exists in the game's code but is inactive. If ads are enabled in the future, this policy will be updated first to describe what ad network is used and what data it processes.

8. Payments and Support

Meridian offers an optional "Support the developer" link to a PayPal.me page. If you choose to use it, that transaction happens entirely on PayPal's website under PayPal's own privacy policy -- Meridian never receives or stores any payment or financial information.

9. Third-Party Infrastructure

Meridian's persistent data (accounts, territory, season history, aggregate stats) is stored in Cloudflare D1, accessed through a Cloudflare Worker. The game server itself runs on Render. Both are infrastructure providers processing data on the developer's behalf, not independent recipients of your information.

10. Children's Privacy

Meridian is a general-audience game and is not directed at children under 13. The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Data Retention and Deletion

Account data is retained for as long as your account exists. To request deletion of your account and associated data, visit the account deletion page or email support@lietastudios.com from any address and include your in-game username.

12. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as Meridian changes. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

13. Contact

Questions about this policy: support@lietastudios.com
